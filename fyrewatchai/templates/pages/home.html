What is the Problem?

8000 forest fires burn 2.5 million hectares just in Canada every year, and this number is only increasing due to climate change and global warming. At this moment, our only way to identify fires are if someone spots them and contacts the local fire department; however, it is usually too late at this point as the spotted fires are too large to extinguish. Therefore, innovation in fire detecting technology is crucially needed.

What is the Solution?

Our AI-based service called FyreWatchAI uses a neural network to spot fires with a 98% accuracy.

What do I need to get Started?

Simply, sign up and you will gain access to our service. Then with an NDVI image and a few coordinates you can use our groundbreaking AI to its full potential.
